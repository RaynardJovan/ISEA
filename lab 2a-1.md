Reflection Questions 

  - Based on the TCO, which printer is the most cost-effective over 5 years?

        Based on the Tco printer office laser is the most cost-effective over 5 years

  - Would the answer change for a home user who prints only 5 pages per week?

        Yes, because If the user only print 5 pages a week the Inkjet would only need 3 cartridges total, making its TCO around $184, whereas the Laser remains over $450 because of the high starting price.

  - What other non-financial factors could influence printer selection? 

        Print speed, print quality, asnd Mainternance and Reliability'
    
  - What cost components are more significant for a large workgroup printer? 

        Paper waste, the cost of toner/ink, maintenance

        The break even of the two printer is 4,8 weeks.
